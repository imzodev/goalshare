# Initial Concept

A Goal Sharing Platform built with Next.js 15, Supabase, and Drizzle ORM, featuring AI integrations, internationalization, and Stripe payments.

## Product Vision

GoalShare is a comprehensive platform designed for individuals seeking personal growth and professional coaches managing clients. It bridges the gap between goal setting and goal achievement by combining structured management with social accountability and advanced AI assistance.

## Value Propositions

- **Social Accountability:** Leverage community sharing and progress updates to stay motivated and committed.
- **AI-Driven Coaching:** Receive personalized, actionable advice and motivational support through integrated AI.
- **Structured Management:** Organize ambitions into manageable goals, milestones, and scheduled activities.

## Core Features

- **AI Coaching Ecosystem:** A real-time conversational chat system for immediate advice and motivation, complemented by automated daily check-ins and habit-tracking prompts.
- **Progress Visualization:** Interactive timelines and visual dashboards to track the journey from inception to completion.
- **Strategic Goal Tracking:** Tools to define, weight, and manage milestones effectively within a unified calendar view.

## Long-term Success

Success for our users means more than just hitting a target; it's about:

- Consistently achieving defined goals and milestones.
- Developing a sustained habit of daily progress and mindful reflection.
- Contributing to and drawing strength from a supportive growth-oriented community.
